we call the day 28 in the month 12 (new year's eve) a 'year day', and that in the month 6 in each leap year a 'leap day'. no other month has the day 28. now we have 28×13+1=365 days (+1 in leap years). these ideas originate from International Fixed Calendar

also, the calendar adds 10000 to the Gregorian calendar year, which is the same scheme as Holocene calendar.
